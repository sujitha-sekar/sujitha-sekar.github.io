About Me

I’m a dedicated Front-End Developer with over 2.7 years of experience, specializing in Angular, currently leveraging Angular 18 to build scalable single-page applications (SPAs). I’m eager to contribute my skills to a dynamic environment and grow further as a full-stack or advanced front-end engineer.
